संहिता सामवेद संहिता Jaiminiya Samhita Jaiminiya Samhita Chapter 01 The Vedas are the source of integral wisdom, science, tradition and culture of a remarkable civilization. They are Oral compilations of distilled wisdom of cosmic knowledge survived from the time immemorial.
